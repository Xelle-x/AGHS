About Us

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2001

Abundant Grace High School is a leading secondary institution in Benin City, Edo State, founded on the principles of academic rigor, moral integrity, and faith-centered education. For over two decades, we have produced some of Nigeria's brightest minds.

Our world-class facilities, dedicated faculty, and holistic curriculum create an environment where every student can discover their potential and pursue greatness with confidence and purpose.
